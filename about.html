Why traveling is important to me

I like traveling because it gives me a break from my normal routine. It also helps me learn new things, even from small moments like trying new food or seeing a new city layout.

When I travel, I try to plan the basics but still leave room to explore, I love not knowing whats next. I love photography, I take pictures of everything I believe is beautiful and so I can remember the trip forever.

Things I usually pack

  • Phone charger and portable battery
  • Necessities like clothes and shoes
  • Water bottle
  • Camera
  • Notes app list of plans
